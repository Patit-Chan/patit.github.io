OUR PUJA

Kolkata is also known for out best durga puja pandels in the whole world .And there are many kinds of pujas done in our kolkata like kali puja saraswati puja and many more.

DURGA PUJA

If you want to explore our durga puja then you need atleast 5 to 6 days to cover all of the pujas in our kolkata .

KALI PUJA

Dakshineswar Kali Temple or Dakshineswar Kalibari is a Hindu navaratna temple in Dakshineswar.

SARASWATI PUJA

In kolkata you can see saraswati puja pandal in every goli. It is one of the famous puja in kolkata.

SHRI RAMKRISHNA

Belur Math is founded by Swami Vivekananda.It is notable for its architecture art and motifs as a symbol of unity of all religions.
